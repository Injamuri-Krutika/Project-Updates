Our Research

The knowing of the unkown

We research about the most unkown thing after our Universe, the human brain. Particulary, we are trying to build computational models that help us understand the human visual cortex. We use DNNs as building block of this computational model.

In this section we list some sources where you will find information about how DNNs can be used to acheive out goal and why they are being used. Some sources also illustrates results supporting the fact that DNNs are indeed the best tools available to model a human's viusal cortex.
